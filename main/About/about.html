About Us

Hi! We are a small and privately owned shop in downtown Chandler. We are located at 71 West Boston Street in Chandler.
At Yes I do Bridal you will experience the best professional service in a family style environment. We don't have too much overhead and we completely understand a bride on a budget, which is why we have a lowest price guarantee. We are here for you and glad to help. Come visit us and get a brand new designer or customized gown without breaking the bank. We specialize in working with curvy and petite brides. Our low cost custom cut and custom length measurement service will save you big on alterations! We do most of the alterations in our store. We also have highly-skilled seamstresses that we recommend off-site. We not only offer bridal gowns, but also have hair accessories, jewelry, shoes, veils, and gowns for the entire bridal party for sale. Serving you is our first priority and we would be honored to be a part of your dream wedding!
